01 — My Journey

My journey.

I'm Jubair Ahamed, an MBA candidate specializing in Logistics and Supply Chain Management at Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute, Chennai. My journey is driven by a passion for building smarter, technology-enabled supply chains that improve the movement of goods and create value across global trade.

With a strong academic foundation in Commerce (B.Com) and advanced studies in logistics, I have developed a keen interest in trade facilitation, port operations, maritime logistics, and digital transformation. I believe that the future of supply chains lies in the seamless integration of technology, data, and operational excellence.

A defining milestone in my journey was my internship at Chennai Port Authority, where I conducted research on the impact of the National Logistics Portal – Marine (NLP-Marine) on trade efficiency. The study was based on primary data collected from 30 professionals across port and logistics organizations, providing valuable insights into how digital platforms are transforming India's maritime logistics ecosystem.

Alongside my logistics career, I have cultivated a strong interest in financial markets and long-term investing. To strengthen my financial knowledge, I earned certifications through the NSE Academy Fin-PRO Program and successfully completed the SEBI Investor Awareness Test (NISM). These experiences have enhanced my analytical thinking, risk assessment, and data-driven decision-making — skills that complement my work in supply chain management.

I am continuously exploring emerging technologies, industry trends, and innovative solutions that are reshaping global logistics. Whether it's supply chain optimization, digital trade platforms, or sustainable logistics practices, I am committed to learning, growing, and contributing to the future of the industry.

This portfolio represents more than my academic achievements — it reflects my curiosity, professional experiences, projects, certifications, and my ambition to build a meaningful career at the intersection of technology, trade, and supply chain innovation.
